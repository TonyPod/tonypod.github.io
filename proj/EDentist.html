The analyzing procedure

 
Our oral disease inference system is based on computer vision and machine learning. Users take a photo of their teeth using our device and upload the photo. In no time, he or she will receive a preliminary diagnosis by our algorithm. Pay attention to it - our algorithm don't want to fully replace dentists, and it is not feasible so far. Our algorithm can roughly determine the severity level by analyzing the photos. With the increasing number of sample images, our algorithm can gain on accuracy.
